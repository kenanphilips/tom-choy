Tom Choy, Award-Winning Real Estate Advisor

Tom is a Real Estate Advisor at Royal Pacific Realty. He was formerly affiliated with Sutton Westcoast from 2002 to 2012. He was awarded membership through sales excellence in Sutton's Presidents Club, Executive Club and Diamond Club. Tom has over 15 years of successful real estate sales experience.

Tom's goal is "to ensure each client's interest above all ." "The client is always No. 1 and not Tom."
